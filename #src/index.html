About company

The OLIMP company was organized to create and implement high-quality and reliable sports equipment for home and light commercial use. Shortage of quality simulators on average price segment led to an unmotivated increase in prices for reliable equipment, creating an additional segment of "semi-commercial" simulators, which brought their cost closer to professional equipment. A filling the middle price segment was formed from the growth in the number of brands and manufacturers of inexpensive simulators with low technical characteristics, by increasing the number of additional options. The main criteria for the release of products with the OLIMP brand are: high quality, reasonable price and comfort of training. Implement this a difficult task was allowed by the tandem of European developments and placement main production in Taiwan. Narrow specialization in cardio equipment for the home allows you to maintain high quality control at all levels production.

Reasonable indicator "price / quality" among those offered by different companies sports simulators in recent years is a rather rare occurrence. We worked hard enough on OLIMP machines to demonstrate a new approach to the release of affordable, beautiful and reliable products. Modern technologies provide comfort and ease of control OLIMP trainers. Numerous reviews from our customers have been received and continue to receive feedback from end users. And now we are we can proudly say that we have taken into account many of your wishes and proposals in updated versions of OLIMP trainers. We are pleased to present you a new generation of our trainers. Now the equipment has become not only “smarter”, more convenient, more comfortable, but also retained the previous price indicators, remaining affordable sports equipment for those who value quality.

The OLIMP concept is based on the following theses:

  • Sports equipment for the home does not differ in efficiency and safety from professional equipment - from the user not requires special training and primary skills, the presence an individual professional trainer, respectively, requires more careful control.
  • OLIMP trainers are easy to use, easy to adapt to individual characteristics of the user, effective and safe, Designed for self-study. The main requirement for simulators under the brand name "OLIMP" is high quality at a reasonable cost - to maintain this balance is the main task professionals from all areas of OLIMP production.
  • The world does not stand still - each generation of equipment corresponds to modern technologies. OLIMP products are manufactured with the latest developments in modern technology, improving and improving popular series of equipment and creating fundamentally new topical models.
  • First of all, OLIMP equipment is focused on the end user - the company is open for dialogue, we welcome all your feedback and comments, this is the main factor in creating a comfortable and convenient products.
  • A quality product is unthinkable without a quality after-sales service - official service OLIMP center provides technical support during the entire period of operation of the simulator, quality after-sales service both during the warranty period and post-warranty period.
